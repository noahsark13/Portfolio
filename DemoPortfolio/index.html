BEAT THE DUNGEON
A linear dungeon runner where the player defeats various waves of enemies, until they reach the boss. After each wave is defeated chests are randomly dropped, each containing randomized weapons and artifacts that grant new abilities to the player. Items that are dropped are based on the tier of chest, that being 3 tiers, common, rare and legendary.
2D unity platformer where the player slays as many enemies and collect as much currency, before reaching the end. This project is meant to be a purely visual demo, where all images and assets are made by hand. However, the gameplay is made using a standard unity template.
